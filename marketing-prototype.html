Features

Everything you need to turn audio into sheet music

🎵

YouTube URL support

Paste any YouTube link — we download the audio and transcribe it automatically

🧠

AI stem separation

Demucs isolates instruments before transcription for cleaner results

🎹

Two-hand piano arrangement

Melody to right hand, bass + harmony to left hand, with intelligent voice assignment

🎼

Interactive sheet music

OSMD renders notation in the browser with playback cursor and Tone.js audio

📄

Publication-quality PDF

LilyPond engraves clean, printable sheet music you can put on a music stand

🎯

Custom piano roll

Color-coded visualization with note labels, beat grid, and tempo-synced playback
